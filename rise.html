SAS: Rise of the Black Swan

Grace Lewis and her mercenaries, the Black Swans, hijack the high-speed train Eurostream while it crosses underneath the English Channel . It's up to Special Air Service member Tom Buckingham to thwart the terrorists while protecting his partner, Sophie.

Cast

  • Ruby Rose as Grace Lewis
  • Hannah John-Kamen as Sophie Hart
  • Sam Heughan as Tom Buckingham
